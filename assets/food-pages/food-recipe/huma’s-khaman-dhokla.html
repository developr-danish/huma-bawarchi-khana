Humaâ€™s Khaman Dhokla (Steamed Gram Flour Cake)

Ingredients:
- 1 cup gram flour (besan)
- 1/4 cup yogurt
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon ginger-green chili paste (optional)
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Salt to taste
- 1 teaspoon fruit salt (eno)
- Water as needed
- Oil for greasing
For Tempering: Bhagaar
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
- A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
- 8-10 curry leaves
- 2 green chilies, chopped (optional)
Instructions:
1. *Prepare Batter*: - In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, yogurt, turmeric powder, ginger-green chili paste, sugar, and salt. - Gradually add water to make a smooth, lump-free batter. The batter should be thick yet flowing.
2. *Steam Dhokla*: - Grease a dhokla plate or a shallow dish with oil. - In a steamer or a large pot with a stand, bring water to a boil. - Just before steaming, add fruit salt to the batter and mix gently. The batter will froth up. - Pour the batter into the greased plate and place it in the steamer. - Steam for around 15-20 minutes on medium heat or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
3. *Prepare Tempering*: - Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. - Add asafoetida, curry leaves, and green chilies. SautÃ© for a few seconds.
4. *Assemble*: - Once the dhokla is steamed, let it cool for a few minutes. - Cut the dhokla into squares or diamonds. - Pour the prepared tempering over the dhokla.
5. *Serve*: - Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and grated coconut (optional) - Serve warm with green chutney or tamarind chutney.
